About Us

Innovative Plastic Solutions Since 2014

Established in 2014 and headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Tasara has built a decade-long reputation for excellence in the plastic materials industry. We specialize in providing premium polypropylene (PP), polystyrene (PS), and polyethylene (PE) materials to manufacturers worldwide.

With strong connections in both domestic and international markets, we've successfully supported major industries including elevator companies, government projects, and private corporations. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable plastic material solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What minimum order quantities do you require?

Our minimum order quantities vary by material type and grade. For standard materials, we typically require a minimum of 1 metric ton. For specialty grades or custom formulations, minimum orders may be higher. Please contact our sales team for specific information about your requirements.

Do you provide material samples before ordering?

Yes, we offer material samples for testing and evaluation. Sample requests can be submitted through our contact form or by directly contacting our sales team. Please note that while most standard samples are provided free of charge, shipping costs or fees may apply for specialty materials or international shipments.

What is your typical delivery timeframe?

Delivery times depend on material availability, order volume, and shipping destination. For standard materials in stock, domestic deliveries typically take 3-7 business days. International shipments generally require 10-21 business days. Custom orders may require additional production time. We provide specific delivery estimates with each quote.

How do your recycled materials compare to virgin materials?

Our recycled materials undergo rigorous quality control to ensure consistent performance. While they may have slight variations in color or specific properties compared to virgin materials, they meet industry standards for their intended applications. We provide detailed technical specifications for all our recycled materials and can help determine if they're suitable for your specific requirements.
